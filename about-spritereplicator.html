What is Sprite Replicator?


Before After

At its simplist, Sprite Replicator can be used to scatter several copies of an image. At its best, Sprite Replicator can be used to create complex patterns and visual effects. By giving the artist full control over the duplication of an image, Sprite Replicator allows for limitless creative possibilities.

Unique Particle Effects


Before After

Sprite Replicator can be used to intricate visual effects such as smoke and magic energy. The Gravity controls add direction to effects such as particle trails and fire.

Blending Control


Before After

Sprite Replicator respects the colors and blend modes of your textures. This allows for much more detail and control than what could be achieved using a brush.
