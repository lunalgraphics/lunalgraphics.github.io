SuperBloom for Photoshop


SuperBloom is coming to Photoshop!


Use SuperBloom to create photorealistic bloom and glow effects, without ever leaving Photoshop.

How to use

Double-click the CCX file to install the plugin with Creative Cloud. You can also install it manually by extracting the folder and dragging it into Photoshop's plugin directory.

To open the plugin, go to Plugins > SuperBloom (beta) > Launch SuperBloom. You will see your document opened in a new SuperBloom window. Customize the settings as you'd like, then click "Finish" to add the bloom back to your document. Since the bloom will be added as a new non-destructive layer, you can add additional adjustments or masks in Photoshop.
