About Us

MISSION

It's been somewhat established as a status quo that digital art is expensive. It requires money, which often must be paid in the form of subscriptions. We want that to change, because we realize that art is the most powerful form of human communication, and we realize that digital art will very soon become engrained in our culture and our identities. We want to allow everyone to be on the front lines of that. Therefore, we are creating free and high quality visual effects tools for digital artists of all kinds to realize their dreams.
