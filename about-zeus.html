Powerful Lightning/Electricity Effects

What is Zeus?


Before After

Zeus can be used to create electric energy effects. That can mean lightning, laser beams, or even lightsabers. Assets created with Zeus can instantly be dropped onto a composite, ready for immediate use.

Powerful Glow


Before After

Zeus uses the same glow algorithm as SuperBloom, creating realistic light falloff. However, Zeus has an additional feature: Glow Distortion, which can be used to create haze or make an electric strand seem even more energetic and unstable.

Beyond Lightning


Before After

The possibilities of Zeus are limited only by your imagination. Zeus can be used to create displacement maps, magical veins, twig patterns, and more.
