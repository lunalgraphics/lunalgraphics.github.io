Zeus

Powerful Lightning/Electricity Effects

What is Zeus?


Before After

Zeus can be used to create electric energy effects. That can mean lightning, laser beams, or even twig patterns. Assets created with Zeus can instantly be dropped onto a composite, ready for immediate use.

Powerful Glow


Before After

Zeus uses the same glow algorithm as SuperBloom, creating realistic light falloff. However, Zeus has an additional feature: Glow Distortion, which can be used to create haze or make an electric strand seem even more energetic and unstable.

Use Zeus Directly in Photopea
