Progen Flares 2 for Photoshop

Installation

  1. Visit the Adobe Exchange listing for Progen Flares 2.
  2. Click "Free" to add it to your Creative Cloud account.
  3. Open the Creative Cloud desktop app and install the plugin.
  4. Restart Photoshop. Progen Flares 2 will appear under Plugins → Progen Flares 2.
