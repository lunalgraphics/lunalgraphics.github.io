Preset-Based Workflow

Color Theater lets you choose from built-in presets to easily start off a color grade, which you can further customize to your liking. You can also make your own presets.

Color Theater has a unique vignette editor which lets you control the size, intensity and color of the vignette effect. It's a great way to add focus and drama to your image.
